About Us
The students of Sree Buddha College of Engineering, Alappuzha are planning to conduct an offline Hackathon named HackBells. This event is based on the concept, "Today's problem will bring tomorrow's innovation". The main aim behind this event is to unleash one's creativity by providing them a perfect platform.
Why HackBells
HackBells is a National Level Hackathon which is open to all students who are enrolled in a recognized university. Encourages female hackers, non-binary and transgenders who belongs to tech community. This brings new learning opportunities and exposure for students in technology.
